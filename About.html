About Me

Student Developer & Designer

Hi, I'm Arjay Sacpa Bugtong, from Buguias, Benguet, currently pursuing my studies as an IT student at the University of Baguio. My favorite color is green, a hue that reminds me of the lush landscapes of my hometown, where the mountains are adorned with vibrant vegetation. Green signifies growth and vitality, echoing the resilience and spirit of the community I come from. When it comes to food, one of my absolute favorites is pinikpikan, especially when served with kiniing. The smoky aroma of the kiniing combined with the savory flavors of the pinikpikan create a culinary experience that never fails to delight my taste buds and evoke fond memories of home.
