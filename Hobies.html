My Hobbies

Jogging is one of my favorite hobbies, since it helps me in many ways to be fit and healthy. When I'm out for a jog, it is as if the noise from the rest of the world fades and only the rhythm of my breathing and stride can grasp my total attention. For me, the time is to relax, be rid of the stress, and enjoy the natural scenery or the city of my own choice. Also, every run feels like having completed something, whether I achieve a new personal record or just finish the route I have been practising for. Jogging is not only an exercise for me, it's a way of life that makes me happy over and beyond what I can describe.
